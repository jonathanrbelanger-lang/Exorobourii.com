Our Purpose.

Pursuing ways to make technology more ethical, more efficient, and more ecologically sustainable.

Ethical

Technology should be a glass box, not a black box. We reject the "move fast and break things" dogma in favor of mechanistic interpretability and radical transparency. We believe that true safety comes from understanding—knowing not just what a model does, but how it thinks.

Without Ego

Efficient

Intelligence should not require a supercomputer. We are dedicated to maximizing "Expressive Bandwidth"—engineering Nano-LLMs and optimized architectures that do more with less. We prove that smart engineering beats brute force scaling.

Without Transaction

Sustainable

The future of AI cannot be built on infinite energy consumption. By solving "Attentional Collapse" and reducing structural redundancy, we create systems that are computationally lightweight and ecologically viable. Sustainable code is the only code that lasts.
