Our Origin

I realized that my personal journey with AI and technology wasn’t available to everyone. Access to these tools is often gated by cost, complexity, or ego.

I wanted to change that. Not for profit, and not for accolades. We do this work because we can, and because we believe that positive change should be transaction-free and extraction-free.
